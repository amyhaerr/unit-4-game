Crystals Collector

You will give a random number at the start ofs the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number generated.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number that was generated.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
